What GitJoker-C2 is

GitJoker-C2 is the operational governance layer of the Hermeticum B.C.E. ecosystem. It sits between human decision and technical execution, enforcing fail-closed validation, responsibility attribution and auditability.

Operational chain

GitJoker-C2 enforces the minimal decision chain defined by Hermeticum B.C.E.

Event → IPR/HRR → DGV → Certified Time → Evidence Bundle
If any required element is missing or unverifiable, execution is blocked.

Core principles

  • Fail-closed by design: uncertainty is treated as risk
  • Human responsibility: every action is tied to a valid IPR/HRR
  • Separation of roles: AI may assist, humans decide
  • Audit-by-design: every decision is reconstructible
  • UE-first governance: aligned with European frameworks

Git as Governance

GitJoker-C2 applies the Git model to governance: versioned decisions, accountable authorship, controlled merges.

No version → no validation → no execution

Relationship with AIJoker

AIJoker provides analysis and decision support.
GitJoker-C2 validates, governs and authorizes execution.

AI can suggest. Only humans decide. GitJoker-C2 enforces the decision.
